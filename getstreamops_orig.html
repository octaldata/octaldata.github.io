get stream ops?

Octal is a discovery, analytics and operations platform enabling businesses to seamlessly control any streaming service, visualize their data, and build custom metrics. Our goal is to give more power to your organization’s data engineering and analytics teams to keep pace with a fast growing data platform.

Unparelled industry experience

Years of development and experience in the field went into creating Octal’s unique and powerful operations and visualization engines.

Operations Engine

Octal’s robust operations engine allows you to effortlessly perform administrative tasks such as creating and deleting topics, managing brokers, and moving data from topic to topiic.

Visualization Engine

Our visualization engine enables you to seamlessly search and visualize data in any streaming platform including Apache Kafka, Redpanda, and Pulsar.

Metrics Integration

Turn opaque information about your streaming data into metrics in real time and connect to your favorite graphing platform

Concrete Security

Integrate Octa's StreamOps with any identity provider via SAML integration and enable robust role based access controls for your team.

Actionable Insights

We applied our years working in the industry and built suggestions and insights directly into Octal. Our insights tool will automatically help you manage your data better.

Our Team's Experience

Our Team

After many years of working in the streaming data world, the team here at Octal realized there must be a more efficient way to manage data. We originally developed Octal for ourselves, in order to solve the day to day operational and administrative problems we faced in the workplace. However, once we began building this tool we started to realize the problems we were solving and our potential audience could be much larger. We decided to share our idea with other people that face the same challenges.

Connect to and administer any streaming service.

With Octal's StreamOps platform you do not have to worry about vendor lock-in. We currently support Amazon Kenesis, Apache Kafka, Apache Pulsar, and Redpanda. Connect to any of these services and administer them seamlessly.

Real Time Discovery Metric Deployment

Create SQL queries on steaming data, save queries, and export them as metrics to your favorite metrics platform including Grafana/Prometheus and Datadog.

Insights Engine

We believe strongly that eveyrone doesn't have the same amount of time to administer their streaming data and tweak it as the data changes. Because of htis We applied our years working in high volume streaming applications to build insights into Octal. Our insights tool will automatically analyze the health and configuration of your topics to help you manage your data better. Once you try our insights engine out and look at the recommendations we present, we feel strongly you'll be hooked.
