Key Features of StreamOps

With StreamOps' robust feature set you can take control of your streaming data. Below is a more detailed list and some screenshots of our features.

Topic Management

Delete and insert records, flush a topic, change replication and partitioning all with the click of your mouse from StreamOp's management engine.

SQL Workbench

Run SQL based queries on your streams just like they were a database or other structured SQL compliant data source.

Insights Engine

Built on our team's deep experiences in the streaming world; get automated recomendations for your topics' and clusters' configuration to help you run more efficiently.
